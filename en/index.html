RED BOX

  • Breaking the Silence, Ending the Poverty.

WHO WE ARE?

Red Box is a Chinese public welfare organization founded by high school students and supported by members of all ages. To increase access to menstrual health education and products to girls in middle and high school in Mountainous and remote areas in China, by providing free products and menstrual education classes.

ISSUE

Due to material and knowledge limitations, many girls in mountainous regions still lack access to menstrual products and related information. Studies show that 500 million women worldwide are affected by period poverty, meaning they face financial burdens just to afford basic sanitary products.

VISION

We aim to reduce period poverty and stigma for girls in Mountainous and remote areas in China and remove educational barriers related to menstruation.

OUR APPROACH

Provide menstrual products, popularize hygiene education, and carry out social activities that advocate for the de-stigmatization of menstruation, including the use of hygiene products and knowledge about menstruation, in order to alleviate and even eliminate menstrual shame.
